This is a simple day counter tool designed to assist you in keeping track of the passage of time within your Minecraft world. With this simple script, you will see the day count displayed in the in-game chat every Minecraft morning. By accurately counting and tracking the number of days that have elapsed, you can effectively manage your resources, schedule various tasks and events, and make informed decisions to optimize your gaming experience. Whether you are a seasoned player or new to the Minecraft universe, this day counter is an invaluable companion that will enhance your gameplay and help you stay organized and immersed in the virtual world.
